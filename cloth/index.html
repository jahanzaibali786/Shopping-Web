Chiffon

Chiffon is a lightweight plain-woven fabric with mesh like weave that gives it transparent appearance. The word Chiffon has a French origin which means a cloth. It is primarily made from cotton, silk or synthetic fibers like nylon, rayon and polyester. Chiffon is most commonly used to weave Sarees, Dresses and Scarves.

Lawn

Lawn cloth is a type of plain weave fabric that is commonly made with cotton. This textile has a relatively high thread count, which provides it with a silky texture. Lawn cloth is usually made using combed yarn, but it can also be made with carded yarn.

Khaddar

Khaddar is a term for handspun and hand-woven cloth from India, Bangladesh and Pakistan primarily made out of cotton. The raw materials may sometimes also include silk, or wool, which are all spun into yarn on a spinning wheel called a charkha. It is a versatile fabric, cool in summer and warm in winter.

Cotton

A soft fluffy usually white material made up of twisted hairs that surrounds the seeds of a tall plant of warm regions and is spun into thread or yarn. Thread, yarn, or cloth made from cotton.

Kurta

Kurta is a long loose shirt, the length of which falls below or may be just above the knees of the wearer. it is a unisex dress that both men and women can wear.
